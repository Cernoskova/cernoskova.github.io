About us

We let you focus on the programme and list of speakers, and we take care of the rest. You will have full control over all aspects of the conference, but without worrying about the little and not so little details that come with scientific events.

Focus on research and development

We are not interested in business meetings or birthdays parties. We organise high-level scientific events, such as conferences, workshops, seminars, hackathons or colloquia. Our clients are:
Senior researchers (PIs or Group Leaders) who organise conferences for research projects
Institutions applying for hosting large international conferences
Researchers with an idea for a new event but with limited funding

Absolute satisfaction of your event participants

All our events are organised to the last detail taking special care on our clients needs and the event participants satisfaction.
